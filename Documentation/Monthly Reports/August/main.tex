\abstract{\addtocontents{toc}{\vspace{1em}}

We present the progress that has been achieved in the development of the project. We are in the process of developing a particle based surface deformation model that is based on shape matching. Said model will be integrated with the Lattice Boltzmann solver, in order to simulate rigid fluids (in our case, body tissue), and their deformations, in real-time. We have also obtained Electromyography data collected from 39 volunteers, and started looking into their processing and analysis.
